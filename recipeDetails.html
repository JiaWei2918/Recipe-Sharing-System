Fish & Chips

A classic British dish of fried fish in crispy batter served with golden fries. Perfect for a quick, delicious meal.

Preparation Time

  • Total: 25 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large cod fillet
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Potatoes, cut into fries

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Add cold water to create a smooth batter.
  3. Dip the cod fillet in the batter and coat it evenly.
  4. Carefully place the fish in the hot oil and fry until golden and crispy.
  5. Fry the potatoes in the same oil until golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels.
  6. Serve the fish with fries and a slice of lemon.
